# What is economics

### Economics: the study of how people seek to satisfy their needs and wants by making choices

### Needs and wants: two of the biggest in shaping the decisions you and I make on a daily basis

### Scarcity: People are faced with limited quantity of goods and service to meet their unlimited wants

### Entrepreneurs: are the individuals responsiable for pulling together the factors of production (land, labor, capital) and create the goods and services we use today

# Trade-offs and opportunity costs

### A trade-off is the act of choosing bewtween two or more options when ganing one means giving up the another

### Opportunity cost is the value of the next best alternative that is given up when a choice is made
